Francisco Gam-ad

My Grandfather

Here's a time line of Francisco Gam-ad's life:

  • 1945 - Born in Guinzadan Bauko
  • 1963 - Finished his Highschool at Guinzadan Natioal High School.
  • 1965 - Started working as a miner here at Baguio City.
  • 1971 - Married the love of his life.
  • 1980 - Built a home at Kamog Sablan Benguet and had 3 sons.
  • 1985 - Moved to his family house with his family for a better future.
  • 1990 - Elected as first Barangay Captain of Guinzadan Sur Bauko, Mountain Province
  • 1996 - End of his service.
  • 2000 - Moved again to Sablan ordered by his first child to manage their business which is house rental.
  • 2015 - Got Sick and need to eat food with no sugar or not salty.
  • 2019 - Died at the age of 74
